About Us

Kinderhaus is a Montessori-inspired preschool guided by the philosophy of Dr. Maria Montessori. We offer a safe, thoughtfully designed campus that encourages independence and joyful learning. Our modern facilities and dedicated educators work together to nurture confident, curious, and happy children.
